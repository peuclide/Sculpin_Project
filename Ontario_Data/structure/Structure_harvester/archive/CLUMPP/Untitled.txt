Where area covered = area of the net in m2 X distance traveled calculated by speed and time.spring = march - jun, summer = jul-aug, fall = sep-nov, winter = dec - febDataset clean-up listcheck all graph click data to make sure not logrithmic - Nick had mixed that up on grossnickle and morgan dataset.lat = approximate latitude (decimal degrees) of samples as indicated in the article.
